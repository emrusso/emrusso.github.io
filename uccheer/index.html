Please donate to help support UC Cheer!

The UChicago cheerleading team receives some funds from the university's sport club allocations as well as from our own fundraising efforts on campus. However, these amounts are not nearly enough to cover the cost of our gear (uniforms, poms, megaphones, etc.) or the cost of attending competitions. Any help you'd be willing to provide would be deeply appreciated. Please leave us your name and address when you donate; we'd love to send you a cheerful thank you note!

You can donate here which will take you to the University's page set up for RSO and Sport Club donations. After selecting the "ORCSA-Student Activities (specify RSO)" option in the "Area of Giving" drop-down menu at the link above, you can earmark the funds for Cheerleading by simply indicating in the "Comment" box of the "Special Instructions" section at the bottom of the page which RSO (i.e. Sport Club) you'd like the funds to go to.

We also happily accept checks made out to "The University of Chicago Cheerleading", which can be mailed to the treasurer for depositing. For more information, contact Daniel Schwartz at cheerleader@uchicago.edu.
